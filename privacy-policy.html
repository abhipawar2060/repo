Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16-07-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Auto_Posts collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our application's integration with Meta technologies (Facebook, Instagram, and the Meta Marketing API).

Information We Access

Our application may access account and advertising information you explicitly authorize through Facebook Login or the Meta Business integration, including but not limited to: your Facebook Page identity, ad account identifiers, campaign, ad set, and ad performance data, and content you choose to publish through our tools (such as images and post text).

How We Use Information

Information accessed through Meta's APIs is used solely to provide the functionality you request — for example, creating or managing advertising campaigns, ad sets, and ads on your behalf, or publishing content to a Facebook Page you manage. We do not sell this information to third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

We retain only the data necessary to operate the requested functionality and for as long as needed to provide our service, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate core functionality (e.g., transmitting requests to Meta's own APIs), to comply with the law, or to protect our legal rights.

Your Choices

You can revoke this application's access to your Facebook or Meta account at any time via your Facebook Business Integrations settings. Revoking access stops any further data collection by this application.
